Hobbit Stew Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs lamb shoulder, cubed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 large carrots, sliced
  • 4 large potatoes, diced
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 1 cup barley
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 1 cup ale
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Meat:

    • In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the cubed lamb shoulder and brown on all sides. Remove the meat and set aside.

  2. Saute the Vegetables:

    • In the same pot, add the onions, carrots, celery, and potatoes. Saute until the onions are translucent and the vegetables start to soften, about 5-7 minutes.
    • Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  3. Combine Ingredients:

    • Return the browned lamb to the pot. Stir in the mushrooms and cook for another 5 minutes.
    • Add the beef broth, red wine, ale, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, bay leaves, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.

  4. Simmer:

    • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the stew simmer gently for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the lamb is tender and the flavors have melded together.

  5. Add Barley and Green Beans:

    • Stir in the barley and green beans. Continue to simmer for another 30 minutes, until the barley is cooked and the green beans are tender.

  6. Finish and Serve:

    • Stir in the peas and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
    • Remove the bay leaves. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Ladle the stew into bowls and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.
